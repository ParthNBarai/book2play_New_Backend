										Privacy Policy Book2Play

Last updated: 10-02-2023



At Book2Play, we take the privacy of our users seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines what personal information we collect and how we use it. By using our app, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect
We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, and phone number.

Location Information: When you use the app, we may collect information about your location to help you find nearby turfs.

Transaction Information: When you book a turf, we collect information about the transaction, such as the date, time, and location of the booking.

How We Use Your Information
To provide the services you request: We use your personal information to set up your account and provide you with the services you request, such as booking a turf.

To improve our app: We may use your information to understand how our users use our app and to make improvements to the app.

To communicate with you: We may use your email address or phone number to send you important updates or notifications about your bookings.

Sharing Your Information
We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

To comply with the law: We may disclose your information if we are required to do so by law or if we believe that it is necessary to protect our rights or the rights of others.

With your consent: We may share your information with third parties if you give us your consent to do so.

Data Retention
We will retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the services you request and to comply with our legal obligations.

Security of Your Information
We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no security measures are foolproof, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or to comply with legal requirements. If we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and notify you through the app or by email.
